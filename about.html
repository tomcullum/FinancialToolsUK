HMRC forgot

FinanceToolsUK makes practical, no-nonsense Excel templates for UK self-employed workers and landlords. We build the tools we wish existed when we started filing our own tax returns.

Why we built this

When you become self-employed in the UK, HMRC tells you to "keep records" — but they don't give you a tool to do it. Most people end up with a messy spreadsheet, a shoebox of receipts, and a panicked January evening trying to figure out what they owe.

We've been there. So we built the templates we wanted — workbooks that handle the HMRC structure for you, auto-calculate your figures, and flag problems before they become expensive mistakes.

Who it's for

Our templates are designed for UK sole traders, freelancers, contractors, and landlords who want to stay on top of their tax obligations without paying for accounting software or a full-time bookkeeper.

If you're comfortable with Excel or Google Sheets and you want something that just works — with correct HMRC rates, MTD quarter dates, and SA105 box numbers baked in — this toolkit is for you.
